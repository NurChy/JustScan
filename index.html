About Us

Just Scan is a powerful tool designed to help developers and security professionals identify vulnerabilities in web applications. Our mission is to make the Internet a safer place by providing an easy-to-use platform for scanning and analyzing websites. With features such as URL scanning and detailed vulnerability reports, our tool ensures that your web applications are protected from common threats such as SQL Injection, Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) and more.
